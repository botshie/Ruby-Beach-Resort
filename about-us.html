About us

A place to remember

Escape to your very own private beach paradise at Ruby Beach Resort, located in the peaceful area of Botoc, Mandangoa, Balingasag. This exclusive resort is designed for one group per day, giving you full privacy to enjoy your most special moments without any interruptions. Whether it’s a wedding, family reunion, birthday party, private gathering, or youth camp, you and your guests will have the entire place to yourselves—just the sea, the sand, and your celebration. Ruby Beach Resort was a dream built by Ruby for the family, and now we’re opening its doors for others to experience the same joy. Our goal is to share this wonderful private space with people who want peace, beauty, and unforgettable memories by the sea. Enjoy clean facilities, wide open spaces, and the natural beauty of the beach—away from the noise, and close to nature.
